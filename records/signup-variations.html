Brands Can Now See Every Question Customers Ask About Their Products
Platform Intelligence Desk • March 2026

A new class of product intelligence is emerging from an unlikely source: the questions customers actually ask. Not surveys. Not focus groups. Real questions, asked in the moment of purchase consideration.

Signetry captures every query flowing through its answer engine and surfaces it to brands in real time. The result is a live feed of customer intent — what they want to know, what's missing from product pages, and where the gaps in brand communication lie.

"The questions customers don't get answered are worth more than the ones they do."

The system works through a single line of embed code. Customers ask questions on product pages and receive verified answers sourced directly from manufacturer manuals — complete with actual diagrams and page references.

But the real value isn't the answers. It's the intelligence. Every query is logged, categorized, and surfaced. Unanswerable questions — where the system can't find verified data — reveal knowledge gaps that brands can act on. It's a continuous feedback loop between customer curiosity and brand knowledge.
