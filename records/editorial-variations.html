Your Customers Have Questions. Your Manuals Have Answers.
A new kind of product intelligence that turns support manuals into instant, verified answers — with actual diagrams.

Every day, customers buy products and immediately have questions. How do I install this? What clearance does it need? How do I clean the condenser? The answers exist — buried in PDF manuals that nobody reads.

Signetry extracts every fact from your product manuals, verifies each one against the source text, and makes them available through a widget that takes sixty seconds to install. One script tag on your product page. That's it.

When a customer asks a question, they get the answer immediately — with the actual diagram from the manual showing them exactly what to do. Every fact is traceable to a verbatim source quote. Zero hallucinations. Zero guesswork.

And you see everything. Every question your customers ask flows into your intelligence dashboard. What do they want to know? What can't you answer yet? That's the intelligence brands pay for.
